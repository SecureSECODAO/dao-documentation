# SECOIN Earning
## Mining
Miners can claim coins based on how many hashes they have mined. They can choose the ratio in which they want to be paid our in SECOIN and SECOREP.

## One time verification reward
Users will be able to claim a bit of SECOIN upon successfully verifying. This verification reward is claimable for each verification separately.

## ABC
Users can swap their WMATIC for SECOIN.

## Liquidity pool
Users can swap for SECOIN in the liquidity pool. Users can also receive SECOIN by providing liquidity to the pool.

## Proposals
Members of the DAO can also create a proposal to withdrawl SECOIN from the treasury.
